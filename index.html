Hi-Lo ~ Card Game



    How to play ~

  1. Click on the set of cards on the left to deal a card.
  2. Decide whether you think the next card to be played will be higher or lower than the current card.
  3. Lock in your guess by choosing either the "Hi" or the "Lo" button on the right.
  4. Click on the set of cards on the left to deal a new card.
  5. Your score will be counted on the bottom, the game will end when you get one wrong or guess correctly 12 cards.

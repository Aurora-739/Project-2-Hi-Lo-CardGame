Hi-Lo ~ Card Game



How to play ~

  • Click on the set of cards on the left to deal a card.
  • Decide whether you think the next card to be played will be higher or lower than the current card.
  • Lock in your guess by choosing either the "Hi" or the "Lo" button on the right.
  • Click on the set of cards on the left to deal a new card.
  • The "dealer" will incriment your scores at the bottom before the next card is dealt. You need to guess 13 cards correctly to win a game.
